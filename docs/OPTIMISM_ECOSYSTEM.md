**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

The Bonsai Registry includes a comprehensive Optimism ecosystem section covering the OP Mainnet, Superchain, and OP Stack. Optimism is an Ethereum L2 optimistic rollup and the home of the Superchain—a network of 30+ OP Stack chains including Base, Mode, Zora Network, and World Chain. Velodrome and Synthetix power DeFi and perps; RetroPGF funds public goods.
