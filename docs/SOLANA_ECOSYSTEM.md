**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

The Bonsai Registry includes a comprehensive Solana ecosystem section covering the high-performance L1 and its thriving DeFi, NFT, gaming, and RWA verticals. Solana is known for sub-second finality, low fees (~$0.00025), and high throughput (65k+ TPS), powering memecoins, NFT marketplaces, and real-world asset derivatives.
