# Web3 Creator Economy — Deep Dive

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Scope:** Music, generative art, NFT creation, publishing, revenue tools

---

## Overview

The Bonsai Registry includes a comprehensive Web3 Creator section covering tools and platforms for musicians, visual artists, and digital creators. These platforms enable direct creator-to-audience connections, on-chain ownership, automatic royalties, and revenue share automation—reducing reliance on traditional intermediaries.

## Key Platforms

**Audius** — Decentralized music protocol; artists retain ownership, earn $AUDIO rewards, access gated content and remix contests. Free for artists and fans.

**Zora** — Permissionless NFT protocol; creators mint with customizable metadata, royalties, and mint formats. Deployed on Base, Ethereum, and OP Stack chains.

**Dassu** — Revenue split automation; smart contract templates, automated payouts, real-time dashboards for musicians, producers, podcasters, and video creators.
