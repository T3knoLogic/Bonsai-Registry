# RWA (Real-World Assets) Ecosystem — Deep Dive

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Scope:** Tokenized treasuries, real estate, credit, art, compliance infrastructure

---

## Overview

The Bonsai Registry includes a comprehensive RWA section covering tokenization of real-world assets—treasuries, real estate, credit, fine art, and luxury goods. RWA tokenization converts physical or traditional assets into digital tokens on-chain, enabling fractional ownership, improved liquidity, and global access. The sector exceeded $185B in 2025–2026, with tokenized U.S. Treasuries leading growth.

## Key Protocols

**Ondo Finance** — Largest tokenized treasury protocol; OUSG (institutional) and USDY (retail) backed by short-term Treasuries. Integrated with BlackRock BUIDL for instant redemptions.

**Centrifuge** — RWA Launchpad for deploying tokenized products (private credit, ETFs, funds) without custom coding. Multi-chain (Ethereum, Polkadot).

**RWA.xyz** — Market intelligence platform aggregating 64+ treasury products, $10B+ TVL. Free and Pro tiers for analytics and API access.
